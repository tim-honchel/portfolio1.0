Operating Instructions

For Administrators:

  1. Register and log in to your account.
  2. Create your store, giving it a name, currency, and business hours.
  3. Add prizes and assign them prices and inventory.
  4. Add students and print debit cards with their login information.
  5. Upload records of student earnings to deposit funds in their accounts.
  6. Receive order reports so you can fulfill purchase requests.

For Students:

  1. Log in with your debit card number and PIN to see your balance, history, and to make purchases.
  2. Select the prizes and quantities you want and add them to the cart.
  3. Review and confirm your order.

Goals

  • Intuitive and exciting user experience for children
  • Automate inventory, balances, and order requests
  • Save admin time on data and store management

User Stories - Students

  • View prizes, prices, and inventory
  • Log in with debit card number and PIN
  • View balance and history of purchases, deposits
  • Add items to cart and approve purchase order

User Stories - Admins

  • Register and log in
  • Create a store
  • Add and modify prizes
  • Open and close the store, manually or on schedule
  • Add and modify students (including deactivation)
  • Upload student earnings
  • Receive purchase orders
